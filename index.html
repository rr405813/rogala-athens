Shelter Shot Sponsor of the Month

The November Shelter Shot Sponsor is "Friends of the Shelter Dogs"
The monthly Shelter Shot Sponsor provides a 5-way vaccination, to each and every dog that enters the Athens Co. Dog Shelter for the entire month. The vaccinations will guard against diseases such as parvo and distemper. If you or your business are interested in being a monthly sponsor for our program please contact FOSDinfo@gmail.com Thank you!

Come Visit Us!

If you have lost a dog it is preferable to come and stop by in person to look at the dogs or at the very least send us a photo for us to compare to dogs in our care.

From the north: Take Route 33 east to the State Route 13 exit. Go thru Chauncey on 13 south. We are approximately 1/2 mile from Chauncey on the right, next to the County Home and Job and Family Services.

County Dog Licenses


ALL dogs in the county must have a license even if they live inside or have a fenced-in yard.
Dog licenses can be obtained from the Athens County Auditor’s office, 15 South Court Street, Room 330, Athens, Ohio 45701. Hours are 8:00 a.m. to 4:00 p.m. Monday through Friday, (740) 592-3226. Dog Licenses can also be obtained from satellite locations. For more information about dog licenses see http://athenscountyauditor.org/dog_license.asp

With money from the dog license fees the county can afford the employees needed to take care of the dogs of the county.
